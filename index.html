Description

Welcome to Mind Logic! This interactive game is designed to test your critical thinking, reasoning skills, and understanding of different logic concepts.

You will be given 4 different topics: Probability, Group Theory, Predicate Logic, and Counting Theory. Each topic contains 20 questions, but only 5 random questions will appear every time you play.

This means that each game is unique the questions will shuffle, so you need to stay sharp and think carefully before answering.

Once you answer all topics, your final score will be shown. To pass the game, you must get at least 70%. If you pass, you can continue otherwise, you can try again and improve your score.
